How to Apply

Admissions to the MetaDynamics School of Engineering are open to a diverse range of applicants, including:

  • Kenyan students through the Kenya Universities and Colleges Central Placement Service (KUCCPS)
  • International students from various educational backgrounds
  • Transfer students from other universities or colleges
  • Diploma holders seeking to upgrade to degree programs
