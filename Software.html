Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering (BSc. Software Eng.)

The Bachelor of Science in Software Engineering is a dynamic four to five-year undergraduate degree program that equips students with the technical expertise, analytical mindset, and creative confidence to design, develop, and maintain high-quality software systems that power today’s digital world. Rooted in the core principles of computer science and engineering, the course provides a solid foundation in areas such as programming, software architecture, data structures, algorithms, and systems design. Students also explore specialized fields like artificial intelligence, cybersecurity, databases, human-computer interaction, and cloud computing. With a hands-on approach to learning, the program emphasizes real-world application through projects, lab work, internships, and collaborative teamwork. Students are trained in the use of cutting-edge development tools, version control systems, agile methodologies, and industry-standard programming languages to solve complex computing problems. As the world grows increasingly connected and digital, software engineers are central to innovation in sectors ranging from finance and health to education, gaming, and mobile technology. This course ensures that graduates are not only skilled coders but also critical thinkers, communicators, and ethical decision-makers in the tech space. Graduates of BSc. in Software Engineering are prepared for roles in software development, application engineering, systems analysis, product management, and emerging fields such as data science and machine learning. The degree also provides an excellent platform for postgraduate studies in computer science, information systems, or tech entrepreneurship. Wherever there’s code, connection, or innovation—software engineers are the architects of the future—and that journey begins right here.

What You Learn

This course builds a strong foundation in both theoretical and applied computing. Students develop skills across key areas such as:

  • Programming Languages: Master core languages like Python, Java, C++, and JavaScript.
  • Software Development: Learn how to build, test, and deploy software systems using agile methodologies and version control.
  • Algorithms & Data Structures: Understand how to optimize performance and solve complex computational problems.
  • Databases: Design, query, and manage relational and non-relational databases.
  • Web & Mobile Applications: Build full-stack apps using modern frameworks and responsive design principles.
  • Cybersecurity & Networks: Learn how to protect digital systems from vulnerabilities and understand how networks function.
  • AI & Machine Learning: Explore how computers learn and make decisions using data-driven models.
  • Human-Computer Interaction: Design intuitive interfaces that enhance user experience.

Practical Application

This program emphasizes real-world learning through:

  • Hands-on coding projects and software development labs.
  • Internships with tech companies and startups.
  • Capstone projects that solve real-world problems using software solutions.

Career Opportunities

Graduates are equipped to work in a wide range of fields, including:

  • Software Development and Engineering
  • Web and Mobile App Development
  • Data Science and Analytics
  • Cybersecurity and Network Administration
  • Artificial Intelligence and Machine Learning
  • IT Consulting and Project Management

Minimum Entry Requirements

To be eligible for admission into the BSc. Software Engineering program, candidates must meet the following minimum requirements:

  • KCSE Mean Grade of B (Plain) or equivalent.
  • C+ in Mathematics and Physics.
  • C in English or Kiswahili.
  • An aggregate grade score of B-(minus) based on the four cluster subjects as recognised by the Kenya Universities and Colleges Central Placement Service(KUCCPS) OR any exception provided by the Engineers Board of Kenya(EBK)
  • Relevant diploma holders as recognised by the departmental senate may apply
